Machine is a 4-month robotics competition hosted by the Quebec Engineering Games (JDG). Every September, delagations receive a package outlining a unique challenge for which they must design and construct a robotic solution that will perform on stage against other schools at the Games in January.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship is a 4-month competition hosted by the Quebec Engineering Games (JDG). Every September, delagations receive a package outlining constraints for the development of an innovative product that addresses a current issue in Quebec. Teams must create a business plan, marketing strategy, financial analysis, video advertisement, and functional prototype of their original product. In Januray, they pitch their product to a panel of expert judges.

Summer Robotics Projects (Mini-Games)

Every summer, our society designs one or more mini-games involving aspects of electrical, mechanical, and software engineering. This initiave allows members to learn and practice hard skills like CAD, 3D-printing, design, programming, soldering, wiring, machining, microcontroller configuration, and more.
